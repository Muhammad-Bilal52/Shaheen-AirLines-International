Disclaimer Recruitment Fraud

  • Our attention has been drawn to the fraudulent activities of persons posing as recruitment agencies or Shaheen Air International Limited (SAI) employees including but not limited to people from the HR department or involved with recruitment/hiring in any way.
  • The fraud involves sending/advertising to people job offers and extorting fees or other charges for this or related purposes. In order to give such frauds an appearance of being legitimate, they may also involve requests to provide personal and other documentation.
  • We would like to make it quite clear that Shaheen Air International Limited never requires any of its candidates to pay for documentation or for other purposes in respect of the recruitment process. If you receive a genuine offer of a job with us you will not be required to pay any money towards administrative fees. We also recommend that you do not disclose your personal or financial details to anyone you do not know.
  • Amongst other precautions to be taken, the public is hereby warned not to communicate with any party except those having a "@shaheenair.com" email address/domain name.
  • Please note that SAI shall in no event be liable for any loss or damage consequent to communication with any unauthorized party.
